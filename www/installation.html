Installation

To install, download the xpi below (right-click, save as) from your browser. Then, in Thunderbird, use Tools->Extensions->Install to install the xpi you just downloaded; restart Thunderbird.

  • quotecollapse-0.2.xpi: Version 0.2 for Mozilla Thunderbird 0.7 - 0.8+ (tested with TB 0.7, 0.8) adds additional functionality with SHIFT, CTRL and CTRL+SHIFT modifiers.
  • quotecollapse-0.1.xpi: Version 0.1 for Mozilla Thunderbird 0.7 - 0.8+ (tested with TB 0.8)
